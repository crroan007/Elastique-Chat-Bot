## Scientific Articles & Clinical Studies

### Cardiac & Exercise Physiology
*   **[Exercise-Induced Cardiac Lymphatic Remodeling Mitigates Inflammation in the Aging Heart](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/acel.70043)** (Aging Cell)
*   **[Lymphangiogenesis contributes to exercise-induced physiological cardiac growth](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2095254622000370?via%3Dihub)** (Journal of Sport and Health Science)

### Movement & Lymph Flow
*   **[Lymphatic flow dynamics under exercise load assessed with thoracic duct ultrasonography](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40275043/)** (Scientific Reports 2025) — thoracic duct diameter and inferred lymph flow increased significantly after moderate exercise.
*   **[Mini Trampoline, a New and Promising Way of SCUBA Diving Preconditioning to Reduce Vascular Gas Emboli?](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35564805/)** (IJERPH 2022) — 15 minutes of mini-trampoline bouncing before dives reduced post-dive vascular gas emboli, pointing to improved fluid/vascular clearance from vertical acceleration.
