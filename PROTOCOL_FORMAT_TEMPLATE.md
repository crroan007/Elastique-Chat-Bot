## Daily Protocol Example

**MORNING: Activation**

- Drink a large glass of water
- 5 minutes of gentle movement (walking or rebounding)
- 10 deep belly breaths

---

**MIDDAY: Maintenance**

- Movement breaks every 60-90 minutes
- Wear compression if sitting/standing for long periods
- Continue hydrating

---

**EVENING: Release**

- Legs Up the Wall for 10-15 minutes
- Gentle self-massage
- Calming breathwork

---

**BEDTIME: Restoration**

- Sleep with legs slightly elevated
- Side-sleeping position for brain clearance
- 5 minutes of 4-7-8 breathing

## Formatting Rules

1. Use horizontal rules (---) to separate major sections
2. Use bold headers for time blocks
3. Add blank lines between each bullet point
4. Format all URLs as clickable hyperlinks using [Text](URL) syntax
5. Group sources by category (Products, Videos, Studies)
